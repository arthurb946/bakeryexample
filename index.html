our story

A small kitchen.
Big appetites.

Nife Bakes is a one-oven, two-hand operation in the heart of Kent. We started baking for friends and family, then friends-of-friends — and somewhere along the way it became a proper little business.

Everything is mixed, baked and decorated by hand the day before collection. No pre-mixes, no shortcuts, no big trays of yesterday's stock.
